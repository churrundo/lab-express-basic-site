Welcome to the Tomas Sankara website!

This website is dedicated to the life, works, and legacy of Tomas Sankara, a revolutionary Pan-Africanist who served as the President of Burkina Faso from 1983 to 1987.

Please navigate the website to learn more about Tomas Sankara, his contributions, his works, and to view a photo gallery of his life and legacy.
